So many chainsaws....


As you were wireing up a grenade launcher up to the mech suit, you noticed a bunch of chainsaws just lying there. This gave the little bunny a idea, he augmented the grenadelauncher in a way that whole chainsaws could be used. With this interesting weapon, the bunny stormed into the house just as the family was having dinner. Starting to shoot out multiple chainsaws in their direction, everyone screamed in horror as their body parts flew of of them. And all they could see, was a sinister grin from a small rabbit.
